WELCOME TO EVERYDAY ENGINEERING LTD, CLAMPING & CONNECTING TECHNOLOGY.

Since it’s formation in 1000 both the reputation of Everyday Engineering and it’s range of material handling products have grown rapidly.

The Company’s technical excellence and commitment to quality were quickly recognised and production capacity steadily increased. Larger premises were acquired in 1984 and these have since been extended to cope with the ever increasing demand for our products.In the early days, Neophix Engineering made only heavy duty hose clamps for the Commercial Vehicle and Earth Moving Equipment manufacturers.

Now the ever widening range of Neophix products are used in many different applications as diverse as Food Processing, Telecommunication, Marine Engines, Nuclear, Mining and Petrochemical industries. Certified to BS EN ISO9001:2008 and the automotive standard TS16949:2008, we also hold several second party manufacturing approvals from our impressive world-class customer base.
